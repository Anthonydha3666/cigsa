02 — INVERSIÓN

The Investment Vehicle

CIGSA operates under a Build-Operate-Transfer model, offering institutional investors structured access to long-duration infrastructure returns.

BOT Model

1

Build

Private capital funds construction of the full corridor — ports, railway, pipeline.

2

Operate

Investors participate in operational revenues across the concession period.

3

Transfer

Infrastructure transferred to the state at end of concession, under agreed terms.

COINGT — The Digital Asset

COINGT is a regulated digital asset backed by real-world assets: land rights and corporate equity within the CIGSA structure.
