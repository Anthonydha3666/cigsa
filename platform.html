PHYSICAL ASSET

Multimodal on Guatemalan territory

An integrated system linking logistics, energy, and industry: a 372 km land corridor, two deep-water ports, double-track railway, and an energy corridor with pipelines and gas ducts.

Deep-water ports

GOVERNANCE

Due diligence advantage: inclusive capitalism

CIGSA is the sole promoter, owner of 100% of the land, and Master Developer of the system. Territorial risk is mitigated by integrating local communities as economic shareholders, not excluded third parties.

Integrated Guatemalan families 3,530

Voluntary territorial access agreements. Local landowners as stakeholders aligned with the project.

  • Eliminates expropriation risk — landowners are shareholders
  • Reduces ESG exposure for institutional investors
  • Documented social license to operate
